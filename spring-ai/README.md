AI is not new but generative AI,the specific branch of AI that uses generative models, known as LLM to produce text,
images, and other content from natural language prompts is what has put the notion of thinking machine into the hands of 
anyone.

`Spring AI`:  
* a framework that enables generative AI capabilities
* it handles all the intricacies of sending the prompt request and handling the response, so the app doesn't have to.
* At the risk of oversimplification, SpringAI is, at its core, a client abstraction for working with various AI providers.
  providing an interface that is consistent across all AI providers and their models making the code you write portable 
  whether your app is backed by models from openAI, Anthropic, Meta...
